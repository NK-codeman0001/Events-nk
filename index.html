13th March ,2019

BLOOD DONATION CAMP

A voluntary Blood Donation Camp is organized on 13th March ,2019 in J.C. Bose University of Science & Technology, YMCA Faridabad ,by the NSS team of university in collaboration with Youth Red Cross Society (YRC) and Bharat Vikash Parishad, Faridabad unit.
The Youth Red Cross(YRC) Society team has reach university at 9:00AM and set all arrangements . Regirstration for blood donation started at 9:30 AM by NSS Volunteers at administrative block of University Finally blood donation started at 10:00 AM in Multimedia Center ( MMC ) of the campus. ... The camp was coordinated by NSS Joint Secretaries and Secretaries under the supervision of Director Youth Welfare Dr. Pradeep Dimri, Deputy Dean Student Welfare Dr. Sonia Bansal and university YRC coordinator ,Dr. Sushil Kumar.
The camp was inaugurated by Industries Minister Shri Vipul Goyal and encouraged the students. He said that students participating in blood donation should share their photos on social media so that it can inspire others as well. On this occasion, Registrar Dr. Sanjay Kumar Sharma, Dean Institutions Dr. Sandeep Grover, Dean Student Welfare Dr. Naresh Chauhan , Dr. Pradeep Dimri, Director, Youth Welfare, Bharat Vikas Parishad President Ashok Goyal, Secretary Dr. Sunil Garg, other officials Raj Kumar Aggarwal, Ajay Aggarwal, SP Mittal and Dinesh Garg were also present.
A number of students , faculty and staff members enthusiastically participated and donated blood . From YRC Society a team of 20 peoples ( including doctor nurse and other staff of parishad) came .And from NSS a team of 22 volunteers work enthusiastically till the end of camp .
Good arrangement of refreshments and hospitality was done by youth red cross society and NSS volunteers . A very caring and amiable hospitality was provided by all the volunteers of NSS.
Prof. Dr. Dinesh Kumar congratulated everyone on the successful organization of blood donation camp. He said that participating in blood donation camp is a part of education in itself, which gives a feeling of 'pleasure of giving' and teaches to come forward to help others. Dean Student Welfare Dr. Naresh Chauhan and Director, Youth Welfare Dr. Pradeep Dimri thanked the officials and medical team of Red Cross Society and Bharat Vikash Parishad for their support in the camp. A 254 units of blood is donated by students and others faculty members of university.

26th August, 2019

JAL SHAKTI ABHIYAN

The NSS team of J.C.B. university of science and technology, YMCA, Faridabad organized a declamation competition and quiz competition on the behalf of “Jal Shakti Abhiyan” ( launched by government of India ) among university students on 26th August, 2019 in the university campus. A total of 39 volunteers and 55 participants were took part in this event. Number of members in each team in quiz competition were three. The topic for declamation was “Do we have enough water” and quiz questions were on general knowledge, environment and water conservation basis.
... An important part of the 'Jal Shakti Abhiyan' is that it focuses on five aspects water conservation and rainwater harvesting, renovation of traditional and other water bodies, reuse of water and recharging of structures, watershed development, and intensive afforestation. The event is leaded by Dr. Pardeep Kumar Dimri and Mrs. Bindu Mangla with the admirable coordination of NSS coordinators, volunteers and staff of different departments.

FIT INDIA MOVEMENT

This program was held in the presence of Dr.Pardeep Dimri (NSS Coordinator), Dr.Sonia Bansal, Ms.Bindu Mangla (NSS Coordinator) and the admirable coordination of the faculty of different departments.
... This Presentation includes India’s indigenous martial art forms,dances and sports. Modi said technology has contributed to a sedentary life style. The campaign is aimed at encouraging people to include physical activity and sports in their everyday life. Our PM made a committee which is headed by Sports Minister Mr.Kiren Rijuju and comprising govt. officials, members of Indian Olympic association, National Sports federations, private bodies and fitness promoters, has been formed to take the movement forward.
